How to Request Account Deletion

We take your privacy seriously. If you wish to delete your Crestal App account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

1. Send a Deletion Request Email

Please email us at: designxcelco@gmail.com with the subject line "Request to Delete My Account". In the body of the email, please include the following:

  • Your account email address.
  • A brief request to delete your account and any associated data.

2. Data Deletion Process

Once we receive your request, we will process it within 7 business days. All personal data associated with your account, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Music preferences and ratings
  • Purchased products and payment history (via Paystack)
  • Other personal details will be permanently deleted.

3. Retention of Data

After account deletion, we will retain some non-personal data for a limited time to comply with legal or financial obligations (e.g., transaction history via Paystack) or if required for system integrity. This data will not be linked to your personal account.

Important Notes:

  • Deleting your account will result in the permanent loss of all associated data, including any saved preferences, music ratings, and purchases.
  • You will no longer be able to access any content linked to your account after deletion.
